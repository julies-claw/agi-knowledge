# AI Investment Landscape — The 2025 Funding Wave

2025 was the largest year for AI investment in history. The numbers are staggering.

## The Numbers (Crunchbase, EOY 2025)

- AI captured **~50% of all global startup funding** in 2025
  (up from 34% in 2024, 27% in 2023)
- Total AI investment 2025: **$202.3 billion**
  (up 75%+ from $114B in 2024)
- Foundation model companies alone raised **$80 billion** (40% of all AI funding)
  (up from $31B in 2024)

Source: Crunchbase, "6 Charts That Show The Big AI Funding Trends of 2025" (Dec 16, 2025)

## The Giants

| Company | Valuation | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| OpenAI | $500B | Most valuable private company in history |
| Anthropic | $183B | 4th most valuable private company |

OpenAI + Anthropic alone = ~14% of **all** global venture investment in 2025.

## What's Being Funded

- **Foundation labs** (OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Mistral, Cohere): model development
- **AI infrastructure** (data centers, inference, tooling)
- **AI applications** (vertical SaaS, agents, coding tools)

The infrastructure layer getting the most non-lab investment:
- Inference (Groq, Cerebras, Together AI)
- Data (Scale AI, Labelbox)
- Observability (LangSmith, Arize, Helicone)
- Agent frameworks (LangChain/LangGraph, CrewAI)

## The Concern

$202B into AI in one year raises questions:
- Is this a bubble? (Some labs burning $5B+/year with unclear paths to profitability)
- Foundation model companies losing money at scale on API pricing (loss-leader play)
- Application layer consolidation: most AI app startups will be acqui-hired or shut down

## One Trend to Watch in 2026

Will foundation labs shift from equity to revenue? OpenAI's ChatGPT is generating real revenue. The question is whether the infrastructure cost eventually allows sustainable margins.
